FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take?

A simple website typically takes 5–10 days. Complex web applications may take 3–6 weeks depending on requirements.

Do you work with students outside India?

Yes, we work with international students. We communicate via email, WhatsApp, and video call. Time zones are not a barrier.

What information do you need to start?

For a website: your goals, target audience, and any brand guidelines. For student projects: your domain, college, and submission date.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Absolutely. We offer post-delivery support for bug fixes, updates, and feature additions for all projects.

Will I get the source code?

Yes. You receive 100% ownership of the source code. We don't lock clients into proprietary systems.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, and international payments. A partial advance is required to begin work.
